# MBASIC Business Programs

Business applications and tools

## Available Business Programs

### Airmiles

Airline distance calculator - calculates great circle distances between 330 cities worldwide using latitude/longitude coordinates (adapted by Jim Lill, 1987)

**Year:** 1987
**Tags:** aviation, distance, calculator, geography

**[Download airmiles.bas](airmiles.bas)**

**Source:** Personal Computing May/June 1977
**Year:** 1977
**Tags:** budgeting, finance, planning

**Source:** Interface Age Feb 1981
**Year:** 1981
**Tags:** project-management, cpm, pert, planning

**[Download cpm-pert.bas](cpm-pert.bas)**

**Year:** 1987
**Tags:** benchmark, performance, factorial, math

**[Download log10k.bas](log10k.bas)**

2. **Open MBASIC** in your preferred UI (Web, Tkinter, Curses, or CLI)

## About These Business Programs

These programs are from the CP/M and early PC era (1970s-1980s), 
preserved from historical archives including OAK, Simtel, and CP/M CD-ROMs.
